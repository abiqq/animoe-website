About AniMoe

Learn more about our platform, our team, and our mission to bring the best anime experience to fans worldwide.

Our Story

AniMoe was founded in 2020 by a group of anime enthusiasts who wanted to create a better platform for fans to discover and watch their favorite shows. What started as a small project has grown into one of the most popular anime streaming platforms, serving millions of users worldwide.

Our mission is to provide high-quality anime content with a user-friendly interface, while supporting the anime industry and its creators.

FAQ

Is AniMoe free to use?

Yes! AniMoe is completely free to use. We believe that anime should be accessible to everyone. We support the industry through ads and partnerships.

How often is new content added?

We add new episodes as soon as they are available, usually within hours of their official release in Japan. Our team works around the clock to ensure you get the latest content.

Can I request an anime to be added?

Absolutely! We welcome anime requests from our community. You can submit requests through our contact form or join our Discord community to suggest shows.

Is there a mobile app available?

Currently, we don't have a dedicated mobile app, but our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers. We're working on an app that will be released soon!
